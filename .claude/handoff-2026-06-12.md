## How to continue

- **Use the expert:** invoke `sj-design-expert` — Builder for decks/motion
  ("make me a pitch deck…"), Reviewer for design reviews/verification.
- **Feed it a new source:** invoke `ingest-design-source <repo-or-url>` —
  manifest-first 5-agent pipeline, Arbiter fan-in, human gate before push.
- **Build a deck manually:**
  `python3 ~/Documents/Development/sj-design/scripts/build_presentation.py spec.json -o out.html`
  then verify per `review-verification-playbook.md` §1 before reporting.
- **Sibling routing:** GPU/WGSL/Metal internals → `graphics-api-expert`;
  game architecture → `game-dev-expert`; Metal load failures → `metal-debugger`.
